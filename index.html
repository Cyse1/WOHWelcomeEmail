Welcome to the Wags of Hope Family!
At Wags of Hope, we believe that every dog deserves a second chance to love and be loved. We rescue dogs from uncertain futures, provide them with the care they need, and connect them with families who will cherish them forever. Your support helps turn heartbreaking stories into beautiful new beginnings — one wag at a time.

🐾 About Our Founder
Wags of Hope started with one small act of kindness — and a heart that couldn’t walk away.

Caroline founded Wags of Hope out of a simple belief: that every animal deserves love, safety, and a second chance.

What began with helping one dog has grown into a mission fueled by compassion, community, and a whole lot of hope. Thank you for being part of this journey — it wouldn’t be possible without people like you.
